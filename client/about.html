About Travel Planner

Connecting travelers with trusted tour planners across Bangladesh.

Our Mission

Travel Planner is a multi-planner marketplace designed to help travelers discover nature, adventure and eco-tourism experiences from verified travel organizations.


Why Travel Planner?

Instead of booking from a single travel agency, travelers can compare packages from multiple planners, view package details, compare pricing and make informed booking decisions.
